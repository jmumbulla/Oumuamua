Column names: Meaning
TGAS.ID: TGAS id
HIP.ID: Hipparcos id
cat: catalog name; ref. Feng & Jones 2017, arXiv:1707.05277
ra: Right ascension (ICRS, Equinox=J2000.0) at Epoch=J2000 [degree]
dec: Declination (ICRS, Equinox=J2000.0) at Epoch=J2000 [degree]
parallax: Trigonometric parallax [mas]
pmra: Proper motion in RA, J2000 [mas/yr]
pmdec: Proper motion in DE, J2000 [mas/yr]
ra_error: Mean error of RA [mas]
dec_error: Mean error of DE [mas]
parallax_error: Standard error in parallax
pmra_error: Standard error in pmra
pmdec_error: Standard error in pmdec
ra_dec_corr: Correlation, ra/dec
ra_parallax_corr: Correlation, ra/parallax
ra_pmra_corr: Correlation, ra/pmra
ra_pmdec_corr: Correlation, ra/pmdec
dec_parallax_corr: Correlation, dec/parallax
dec_pmra_corr: Correlation, dec/pmra
dec_pmdec_corr: Correlation, dec/pmdec
parallax_pmra_corr: Correlation, parallax/pmra
parallax_pmdec_corr: Correlation, parallax/pmdec
pmra_pmdec_corr: Correlation, pmra/pmdec
rv: Radial velocity [km/s]
erv: Standard error in rv [km/s]
qrv: quality of rv from the literature (For xh and xt objects, from good to bad: A, B, C, D; for rt and rh objects, the value is StdDev_HRV in RAVE DR5, representing the standard deviation in HRV from 10 resampled spectra)
tenc: Encounter time [Myr]
tenc.5per: 5% quantile of tenc [Myr]
tenc.95per: 95% quantile of tenc [Myr]
denc: Encounter distance [pc]
denc.5per: 5% quantile of denc [pc]
denc.95per: 95% quantile of denc [pc]
venc: Encounter velocity [km/s]
venc.5per: 5% quantile of venc
venc.95per: 95% quantile of venc
